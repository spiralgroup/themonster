Completing the Order Form

1. Your Small Business Information. Once you have selected your business category, you will be taken to our secure online order form to continue the process of creating your new Spiral Group, Inc. 4. Your Net Proceeds. These are the monies you will receive after all costs and expenses are deducted from the Gross Proceeds arising from the sale of your products and services via your Spiral Group, Inc. Small Business Showcase WebPages Merchant Services account. You can choose to have these monies delivered through the USPS via check or deposited directly into your bank account.
