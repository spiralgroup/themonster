mystery shops

We design and implement Mystery Shop Programs that provide honest and in-depth views from the customers' perspective. We compile and analyze data from completed evaluations to gain awareness about how it affects your small business, operationally.

Our insights include wait & service times; impact of workforce training; quality of products & services; suggestive selling techniques; and parting statements.

Mystery Shopping Program

A Mystery Shopping Program can be invaluable for evaluating and improving the performance of your staff. Below are the steps used to create and execute your customized Mystery Shopping Program.


1. Plan

We begin by getting to know your small business and operational structure so we can devise a custom mystery shopping plan and create a specific timeline of execution. The cost to conduct each Mystery Shop is determined during this step.


2. Schedule

Next, we schedule certified shoppers to perform your specific tasks, such as purchasing & returning items, calling your customer service line, using your website, or asking questions of your associates. After discretely completing your tasks, we move on to the next phase.


3. Evaluate

During this phase, the shopper completes your customized questionnaire. Answers are a combination of multiple choice or scored points, along with highly detailed comments that allow you to fully understand the overall customer experience.


4. Analyze

We analyze data from completed evaluations to gain insight into how it affects your small business, and present you with a view of your operation through the eyes of the customer. We offer recommendations on how to honor stellar performance, or improve where needed.


5. Improve

We guide you on how to improve operational strategies using what we learn from the evaluations. Your new knowledge of problem areas will help you create noticeable changes that will delight your customers.


6. Repeat

We will work with you to create a schedule of ongoing evaluations and other customer experience research services that will help fine-tune your brand experience and take your small business to the next level.
