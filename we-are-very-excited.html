USEFUL TIPS

This page contains a list of useful tips that may help us both get the most from your consultation.

useful tips

BE PREPARED

Know what you want to accomplish during your consultation. Have specific issues you'd like to discuss and questions to ask. Be ready to present any facts or figures that would shed light on your current operational situation.

Being prepared with an objective will decrease the likelihood of time being spent on issues that don't really need to be discussed during your free phone session. This way, we will be more inclined to get to the core of your most pressing business concerns. Keep in mind however, if you require more time to discuss your situation, your consultant will be more than happy to oblige.


REVIEW OUR WEB PAGES

If you haven't done so already, review the contents of the following web pages before your consultation to determine if our products and services can be used to achieve your business objectives: BUSINESS ASSESSMENT TOOLS and ONLINE MARKETING SOLUTIONS — SMALL BUSINESS SHOWCASE WEBPAGES & THE SG LISTING SERVICE.


DETERMINE AFFORDABILITY

Before your consult, please review our prices to determine affordability. If you decide to use our products and services after your consult, then check out our SPECIAL OFFERS.


TAKE NOTES

During the consult, take notes on any overarching themes that come out of the conversation from both you and your consultant.


SAVE TIME

Although you may have a clear objective in mind, your Business Consultant may feel the need to address certain items, as they pertain to achieving and maintaining operational excellence. Please save time for useful and ample feedback.


CREATE ACTION ITEMS

Close the consultation with one clear objective in mind, to create action items. These should be specific and have solid deadlines. This will allow you to begin the process of putting to use what we both discover during your phone session.
