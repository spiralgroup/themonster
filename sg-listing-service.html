Frequently Asked Questions

Why not use an automated service?

Well, it has been our experience that automated systems are basically a scam. Here's why: Small businesses have to pay a monthly fee for the use of a dashboard that contains all kinds of bells and whistles. These providers would like you to believe that paying to use their so-called patented technology, via a dashboard, will put your mind at ease when it comes to claiming citations, updating your business information, and managing your 50 or more "business listings".

Small business owners still have to manually do the work of maintaining their listings, and pay monies to an organization for the work they have to do themselves simply because they have been provided with a dashboard. I hope you are paying particular attention to how many times the word "dashboard" is being used. It is the main selling feature. In a nutshell: I have to pay a company a monthly fee for the use of their dashboard to maintain my business listings.

We have also discovered that by listing your small business in ONLY the major online business directories, your small business is automatically listed in other reputable online business directories. And, you don't need a dashboard for that.

We have also discovered that by listing your small business information in some of these free and unheard of directories, it only leads to more "scam-likely" phone calls to your business phone, and unwanted emails to your business email account. Don't take our word for it - give it a try. As soon as you begin listing your small business information in some of these free online directories that no one has ever heard of, you will notice an increase in automated phone calls and hang-up calls. More importantly, when you attempt to return the call using the number on your phone's caller ID, you will notice that some of the telephone numbers are non-existent.

Some of these larger organizations (Yext) have created some of these so-called free online business directories as a ruse to get unsuspecting business owners to merely provide their information so it can be sold to others who purchase email lists, phone lists, etc.

We have also come to the inescapable conclusion that MOST people who are conducting online searches are actually using Bing and Google. Think about it - when you conduct an online search, what do you do? Do you Google a thing and Bing a thing? Or, are you using skulduggery.com and this-is-a-set-up.org?

I have personally spent the past two years researching how these companies operate, and this is how I am able to share this information. We manually list your business, but leave it up to you to enhance your business listings. It is a lot of work, but just getting started by getting listed is 90% of the local listings battle, which is what the SG Listing Service offers.

We have also realized that when your small business information changes - and it does because we are constantly shaping our business models to better serve the needs of our local markets - all you have to do is update your information through the 8-12 original online business directories where we list your information through the SG Listing Service.

No matter what, as small business owners, we are responsible for making sure our business information is always accurate and up-to-date. This can only be done manually. Once your small business has grown to the point where you can afford to pay $100 - $500 a month for a dashboard where you have to manually input your business data anyway, then I encourage you to do so. Until then, the best way to ensure accurate, up-to-date business listings is to set aside time in one work day to attend to these administrative tasks and you will be surprised at how quickly this can be accomplished if you can consider listing your small business with only the most reputable service providers.

The online directories where we list your small business information are absolutely free, but since we go through the process of listing your business information, manually, we charge a one-time fee for this service. In addition, the SG Listing Service is included when you join the SG MarketPlace. For all other small business owners, we charge a onetime fee of only $99.99. We understand what it takes to run a small business and our goal is to help other small businesses succeed by providing the most economical business solutions. We understand the importance of having your small business listed in reputable online business directories, and we have also experienced and seriously endured the scams.
