Buy Online, Pickup In-Store

Instruct customers on how to retrieve online purchases from your retail location. We've provided the following Buy Online, Pickup In-Store (BOPIS) example.

BOPIS INSTRUCTION EXAMPLE

Select items and proceed to online checkout. Select "In-Store Pickup" from the available delivery methods for each item.

Once your order has been processed, you'll receive an email notification. Typically, orders are ready for pick-up 1 hour after being placed. Only the purchaser or designated pick-up person can retrieve the purchased item(s).

Orders will be held for 7 days. If not picked up after 7 days, we'll cancel the order and refund your card at the end of the holding period.
