Buy Online, Pickup In-Store

Instruct your customers on how they are to retrieve their online purchases from your retail location. We have provided the following Buy Online, Pickup In-Store (BOPIS) example.

BOPIS INSTRUCTION EXAMPLE

Once you've chosen your items, you'll go through our regular online Checkout process to purchase them. Select "In-Store Pickup" from the available delivery methods on the order form for each item you are purchasing.

Once your order has been processed, we will send you a notification email when your items are ready for pick up. Orders will be ready for pick up 1 hour after being placed. The person who placed the order can pick up the items. However, an alternate pickup person can retrieve your purchased items only if you call us soon after making your purchase and providing the designated individual's full name and email address. Only the purchaser or designated pickup person can pick up the order.

Orders will be held at the store for 7 days. If you have not picked up your order after 7 days, we will cancel the order and refund your card at the end of the holding period.


You Will Need:

• Store Pickup Notification Email.
• Valid photo ID.
• Credit card used for purchase.


Your Designated Person Will Need:

• Store Pickup Notification Email.
• Valid photo ID.


If you have questions, call 773-242-0705.
