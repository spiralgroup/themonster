about us

who we are

Spiral Group, Inc. is a not for profit organization located in Chicago, Illinois. In addition to our Small Business Showcase WebPages, we offer affordable Business Excellence Tools to locally under-served Small Businesses. We're committed to assisting this population of entrepreneurs by leveraging our skills and many years of corporate experience to help them build reliable companies that can support the needs of their markets.

the ceo

Michelle Jones is the Founder and CEO of Spiral Group, Inc. She began developing the company's business model after serving time in a correctional facility. Her goal is to make useful contributions to the Small Business Community and other returning citizens.

In 2016, Michelle created a free, online Employment Preparation Training Program for job seekers with criminal histories. She is dedicated to helping returning citizens in achieving self-sufficiency, while reducing an inclination toward recidivism.

Minister Jones

Michelle became an ordained minister in April 2007. In lieu of a traditional church, she decided to execute her ministerial duties as a web developer and operations consultant. She targets locally owned small businesses she believes are under-served when it comes to an affordable online presence, marketing services, compliance training for their teams, and the business tools that ultimately lead to perpetual operational excellence.

Michelle has many years of corporate experience, and has worked with the following brands: Subway, Portillo's, Quiznos, Dock's Great Fish, Burger King, Chili's, Target, and Jeepers. She has over 20 years' experience in the hospitality and food-service industries as an hourly worker, cashier supervisor, assistant manager, general manager, trainer, training manager, and operations consultant.

She opened her first business, Restaurant Materials Resource Center, in 1999 where she designed business documents, labels, and posters for the food-service industry. She is a Certified ServSafe® Instructor and Registered ServSafe® Examination Proctor.
