Program Requirements

Google Chrome. If necessary, click here to download the latest version.

Adobe Acrobat Reader. If you don't already have it, get it because you'll need it to complete this online training program. Don't worry - it's free. Click here to download.

Library Card. If you don’t already have one, your town’s local public library will be glad to help. All you’ll need is a current, unexpired government issued ID card. The purpose of acquiring a library card is to be able to use the public computers in the library, and to be able to borrow a Wi-Fi hotspot if you should ever need one. Once you see how wonderful your local library is, you’ll realize that having a library card is priceless.

Business Email Account. Of course you have an email account. However, look at it carefully and decide if it’s giving potential employers, or other professionals, a positive impression about you and your personal brand. If it makes you think twice, then it’s probably a good idea to create a more professional email address.

Cumulative Exams

The cumulative exams are your required homework assignments. You must pass each exam with a score of 100% to be deemed Job Ready and receive a Certificate of Completion for each course. A link to each course exam can be found on the Table of Contents page.

Frequent Communications

Check your inbox frequently, because you will begin receiving regular communications from the Program Administrator. The purpose of this is to check your overall progress and gauge your readiness for sustainable employment. How and when you respond to these communications will be taken into consideration, and accounts for 75% of your overall grade. Why? Employers of choice require you to respond to all their communications promptly and professionally.
