demo about us

Spiral Group, Inc. is a not for profit organization located in Chicago, Illinois. In addition to these Small Business Showcase WebPages, we offer Business Excellence Tools and local advertising solutions to under-served Small Businesses throughout the Greater Chicagoland area. We work with our clients to help them refine operational infrastructure by establishing lean systems & processes, and the outsourcing of non-core business tasks. As a result, they enjoy reduced operating costs and improved workforce productivity.

Our commitment to assisting other entrepreneurs take their small businesses further is unparalleled. By leveraging our skills and many years of corporate experience, we help them build reliable companies that can support the needs of their markets.
