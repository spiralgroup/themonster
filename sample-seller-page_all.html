About Us

who we are

Our founder had an idea to create an online transactional marketplace that was more than a place that served affordable and innovative ad products and business excellence tools. She wanted to give locals a place where they'd feel welcome, could get amazing deals at down-to-earth prices, and where they'd be treated right by people with a passion to serve. We're loved for our hand-crafted ad products, business listing service, and SG MarketPlace Seller Pages. Learn more here.

The culture at SG MarketPlace was instilled by Michelle long before a mission statement was ever developed — providing stellar service is our key to success. It's what makes all our guests feel right at home. So, join us for a fun and colorful time. But most of all, achieve the desired outcome for local marketing campaigns by preparing your teams and operations for welcoming and servicing new customers.
