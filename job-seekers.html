Join Our Team

Unlock Your Potential. Join The SpiralGroup Sales Team.

We seek energetic, self-motivated, and ambitious people to join the Spiral Group team as Sales Professionals. Persons considered hard to employ, due to a criminal past, are strongly encouraged to apply. Ideal candidates have exceptional service skills and the ability to follow very specific instructions to complete all tasks.

Your Responsibilities
Identify small businesses that would benefit from our products and services. Contact potential customers and present our products and services in an attempt to sell to those customers. Obtain necessary information from the customers in an effort to better serve them. Set follow-up appointments to help complete sales.

The Requirements

  • Complete our online training program with 90% - or higher - within the time allotted.
  • Cell phone and reliable transportation.
  • Previous experience in sales, customer service, or other related fields.
  • Ability to build rapport with clients.
  • Ability to think on your feet to show potential returns for customer.
  • Familiarity with Internet terms and use.
  • Stellar verbal skills and the ability to work collaboratively in a team-setting.

Your Salary
This is a strictly commission-based sales position. You will be working as an independent contractor. We are launching new products and services that will help drive orders and your commissions. Confident and professional Sales Reps earn --% commission on all products and services sold to clients.

What We Offer

  • Product knowledge training
  • Sales Support
  • Customer Service and Technical Support
  • Marketing Materials
  • A great working environment
  • Certificate of Excellence for successfully completing our training program
  • A professional profile on our website and business email address.

We know it takes a special person to work in sales. Therefore, we are committed to providing our people with an enriching and rewarding environment. We provide the resources, tools, and support our employees need to serve our clients in the best way possible.

Personal growth and development is important to us, and while our performance expectations are significant, so is our dedication to each individual's success.

Spiral Group, Inc. is an Equal Opportunity Employer committed to workforce diversity. We believe in a diverse and inclusive environment, one that is grounded in our dedication to the Small Business Community. Respecting, nurturing and encouraging diversity of thought, background and experience contribute to positive work environments that result in exceptional customer service and increased profitability.
