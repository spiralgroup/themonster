LEARN HOW TO COMPETE IN TODAY'S JOB MARKET.

Spiral Group's Online Employment Preparation Training Program was designed to teach Job Seekers how to compete in today’s labor market. Participants learn how to set realistic occupational goals; how to conduct occupational research; and how to develop a positive personal brand. As a Training Services Provider, our main goal is to help Job Seekers improve their organizational, decision-making, customer service, and soft skills. Upon successful completion of this program, participants are deemed JOB READY and receive a Certificate of Completion that can be used alongside their other self-marketing materials.

ENROLL IN OUR ONLINE TRAINING PROGRAM TODAY.

Job Seekers who enroll in Spiral Group's training program will receive our EMPLOYMENT PREPARATION TOOL-KIT® for only $39.99. Using this tool-kit, along with the Interview Preparation Resources below, Job Seekers will learn how to make effective use of the skills they already possess and how to communicate these to potential employers. We also offer instruction in telephone etiquette and how to request references from individuals who can attest to a Job Seeker's character and employability.

SHAPING TODAY'S JOB SEEKERS FOR EMPLOYERS OF CHOICE.™

Although anyone having a desire to improve their skills can benefit from this training program, it was originally designed for those with criminal histories. Michelle A. Jones [a twice convicted felon] is the Founder of Spiral Group, Inc. The not for profit organization provides Employment Preparation Training for persons who are willing to make a commitment to positive change. Our goal is to assist returning citizens in achieving self-sufficiency, while reducing an inclination toward recidivism. We are dedicated to helping this population of job seekers overcome many obstacles to employment by improving their organizational, time-management, decision-making, interviewing, and soft skills.

EMPLOYMENT PREPARATION TOOL-KIT® CONTENTS

0 | ORIENTATION COURSE
Investing in your Professional Development is time spent improving your Self, overall. Taking responsibility for your life, circumstances, and career path will tend to be automatic. You will begin employment seeking activities with confidence.

1 | PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT COURSE
This course is comprised wholly of Soft Skills modules—critical thinking, handling workplace conflict, the art of active listening, mindful decision-making, customer service basics, body language, etc. Developing this skill-set is crucial for promoting one’s success in the workplace.

2 | CAREER DEVELOPMENT COURSE
In this course, participants learn how to conduct occupational research, set occupational goals, and how to begin the process of creating a positive personal brand. There’s also an introduction to entrepreneurship so that interested participants can begin the process of becoming small business owners.

3 | DOCUMENT PREPARATION COURSE
This course was designed to teach Job Seekers how to develop their own self-marketing materials—resume, cover letter, job reference page, etc. We believe that creating their own documents allows Job Seekers to know who they are, the skills they possess, and what they can offer an employer. This process promotes self-reliance and confidence.

4 | STRATEGIC INTERVIEW PREPARATION COURSE
In this course, we engage participants in modules such as, telephone etiquette for job seekers, strategic interview preparation, appropriate interview attire, and following up after the interview. This course also includes the module 'Landing a Job with a Criminal Record'; it is intended to serve as a guide so that the chances of landing a job with a criminal history may increase.

5 | PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT COURSE
This course is presented from the perspective that our 'job-ready' participants have acquired employment by convincing the hiring manager of their exceptional capabiities; their high levels of professionalism; their ability to execute the duties of the position; and how they will begin to benefit their new employer as soon as they are allowed to contribute to the organization. This course was designed to teach students how to deliver on their promises.

AFTER COMPLETING THIS TRAINING PROGRAM, THE JOB SEEKER SHOULD BE ABLE TO:
Understand what it means to be JOB READY.
Demonstrate critical thinking, active listening, mindful decision-making, customer service, telephone, and appropriate body language skills.
Conduct occupational and employer research.
Begin the process of creating a positive personal brand.
Develop their own self-marketing materials—resume, cover letter, job reference page, etc.
Understand who they are as a candidate and what they can offer an employer.
Address their criminal past with poise, ease, and tact.
Follow up appropriately after any job interview.

WHO SHOULD ENROLL
Job Seekers of all kinds, especially those who have criminal histories.
