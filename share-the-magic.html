Can these ads help achieve my goals?

Absolutely. Our Smart Promotion Ads can help your small business generate awareness of your products or services, promote seasonal items, or create more demand for a best seller.

The goal of our Scrolling Footer Logo Ads is to help you establish credibility and brand identity with our marketplace shoppers. These ads allow you to connect with them intellectually and emotionally so they're motivated to take action when they see your Smart Promotion Ads.

Providing opportunities for small business owners to promote their products and services in our marketplace search results for only $1 a day was a real no-brainer. If you haven't already tried the search engine on our site, we encourage you to do so and imagine your small business ad located in that coveted spot. Who doesn't want to be number one on Google?

Where will these ads appear?

Smart Promotion Ads appear, prominently, throughout the SG MarketPlace. Click here to see exactly how and where these ads will be placed. Scrolling Footer Logo Ads appear in the footer section on all seller pages within the SG MarketPlace. Google Keyword Search Ads appear on every webpage with a search box.

Are these ads intrusive?

Certainly not. Our Smart Promotion Ads are non-intrusive and can be easily dismissed by clicking on the gold ad icon conveniently located above the ad-box. Customers interested in learning more can click on your ad and be presented with your advertisement inside the ad-box. These ads will never interrupt the flow of a visitor's stay on our website.

Because our Scrolling Footer Logo Ads are located in the footer section throughout our marketplace, they are merely a fun, colorful, and interesting distraction that can lead to increased awareness and sales for your small business.

Who creates/designs the ads?

We do! All we need from you is the information we request on our online order form.

What if I don't have a logo?

If you don't have a logo for your small business, we will create a temporary for free. If you decide to keep it, wonderful!

What if I have an SG Seller Account?

If you have an SG Merchant Account, do not order ad products using the online order forms. Instead, contact your Account Manager and he/she will place an order for you.
