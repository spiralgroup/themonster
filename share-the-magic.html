Frequently Asked Questions

What is brand advertising?

Brand advertising is a form of advertising used to establish connections and build strong, long-term relationships with consumers over time.

Can these ads help achieve my goals?

Absolutely. Our Branded Category Ads can help you generate awareness of a new product or service, promote seasonal items, or create more demand for a best seller.

How much do these ads cost?

Our Standard Style is $99 per month, and Premium is only $139 per month. Place an order using our secure online order form. You can choose your preferred payment method on the order form. After we confirm the details of your ad by phone, we will send you an invoice for payment via email. If you have questions, call us at 773-242-0705. We're open everyday from 8am to 8pm.

Where will these ads appear?

Your Branded Category Ads will appear, prominently, at the top of every qualifying webpage throughout the SG MarketPlace. Although we have shown you the two styles we offer above, click here to see exactly how and where these ads will be placed.

Are these ads intrusive?

Certainly not. Our Branded Category Ads are non-intrusive and can be easily dismissed by clicking on the blue ad icon conveniently located outside the ad-box. Customers interested in learning more can click on your brand logo and be presented with your advertisement inside the ad-box. These ads will never interrupt the flow of a visitor's stay on our website.


Have an SG Merchant Account?

If you have an account with us.

If you have a Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account, you do not need to order ad products using the order form. Instead, contact your Account Representative at 773-242-0705 any day of the week from 8am - 10pm.
