FAQ's

Can these ads help achieve my goals?

Absolutely. Our ad products can help your small business generate awareness of your products or services, promote seasonal items, or create more demand for a best seller. They can be used to help you establish credibility and brand identity with our marketplace shoppers.

Where will these ads appear?

Both our Smart Promotion and Scrolling Footer Logo Ads appear, prominently, throughout the SG MarketPlace on all business listings pages. SFLA's appear in the footer section on each SG MarketPlace Seller Page.

Are these ads intrusive?

Certainly not. Our Smart Promotion Ads™ are non-intrusive and can be easily dismissed by clicking on the "close this ad" button conveniently located at the bottom of the ad-box. Whenever the web page is refreshed, these ads are restored.

Customers interested in learning more can click on your ad's image and be presented with your advertisement inside the ad-box. These ads will never interrupt the flow of a marketplace shopper's visit to our website.

Because our Scrolling Footer Logo Ads™ are located in the footer section on seller pages, they are merely a fun, colorful, and interesting distraction that can lead to increased awareness and sales for your small business.
