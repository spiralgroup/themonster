Frequently Asked Questions

Can these ads help achieve my goals?

Absolutely. Our Exclusive Category Ads can help your small business generate awareness of a new product or service, promote seasonal items, or create more demand for a best seller.

The goal of our Scrolling Footer Logo Ads is to help you establish credibility and brand identity with our marketplace shoppers. These ads allow you to connect with them intellectually and emotionally so they're motivated to take action when they see your Exclusive Category Ads.

Where will these ads appear?

Exclusive Category Ads appear, prominently, on the SG MarketPlace business category web page of your choice. It is exclusive, so there are no other ads competing with your ECA. Although we have shown you the two styles we offer above, we invite you to click here to see exactly how and where these ads will be placed.

Scrolling Footer Logo Ads appear in the footer section on every page throughout the SG MarketPlace, including Seller Pages.

Are these ads intrusive?

Certainly not. Our Exclusive Category Ads are non-intrusive and can be easily dismissed by clicking on the blue ad icon conveniently located outside the ad-box. Customers interested in learning more can click on your ad and be presented with your advertisement inside the ad-box. These ads will never interrupt the flow of a visitor's stay on our website.

Because our Scrolling Footer Logo Ads are located in the footer section throughout our marketplace, they are merely a fun, colorful, and interesting distraction that can lead to increased awareness and sales for our advertisers.

Who creates/designs the ads?

We do! All we need from you is the information we request on our online order form.

What if I don't have a logo?

If you don't have a logo for your small business, we will create a temporary for free. If you decide to keep it, wonderful!
