How It Works


touch_app Choose Your Plan


Please review each plan carefully to ensure it meets your current business needs. All plans include showcase pages maintenance & dedicated customer support. Once you've selected a plan, you will automatically proceed to Step 2.

important_devices Choose a Category


The SpiralGroup MarketPlace has 12 business categories, listed in alphabetical order. For complete details about a single category, tap on the category label. Once you've decided on a category, tap on that category's image so that you can proceed to Step 3.

description Provide Your Business Details


In this Step, you will need to provide the pertinent details of your small business. Please fill out the form, in its entirety, to avoid delays with publishing your showcase pages.

perm_media List Your Showcase Items


In this step, list the items you want showcased. If you don't have product images, please inform your SpiralGroup Specialist. You may be able to borrow from our stock photos.

shopping_cart Proceed to Checkout


In this step, you will provide your payment information using our secure PayPal account. If you don't have a credit or debit card, give us a call so that we can discuss other payment options.

account_box Get Verified


We will review your information for compliance with our guidelines, and then contact you by phone to confirm and verify the data. Once approved, your showcase will go live within 72 hours.
