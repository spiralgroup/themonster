How do I update my SBS WebPages?

Once you become a Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant, just submit any updates to your Account Representative via email. He/she will let you know how long it will take to see those changes reflected on your web pages - usually within 24 hours. All plans include updates, maintenance, and support.

How will customers find my SBS WebPages?

Customers will be able to discover your Small Business Showcase WebPages when searching for Spiral Group + your business name.

How will you promote my small business?

Each SBS WebPages Plan includes a Scrolling Footer Logo Ad. Your Scrolling Footer Logo will appear on all our Small Business Showcase WebPages within the same and complimentary business categories.

Our Premium Plan includes the SG Listing Service. We manually register your small business in the top eight electronic business directories. Among them are Bing, Google, Yelp, YP.com, and Foursquare.

Our Premium Plan also includes a premium style Branded Category Ad, which will run for 90 days. You can feature up to six of your products and services with our scrolling app. These ads will appear, prominently, at the top of every qualifying webpage throughout the SG MarketPlace.

How much does it cost to run LM Campaigns?

How do I update my appointment availability?

You can update your bookable hours whenever you choose. Each SBS WebPage contains a link for your customers to book online appointments through Calendly, when they click on your menu-awning. When we set up your SBS WebPages, we also set up your free account with them; therefore, you create and update your own availability.

Am I a good fit for in-store pickup?

As long as you have a non-residential/non-industrial storefront where a customer can pick up his or her products, then an in-store pickup campaign could be right for you.

How are returns handled?

We follow whatever the standard return policy is for PayPal. You can review the details in our Merchant Agreement.

How is feedback collected from customers?

When customers click on your menu-awning, there's a link at the bottom of the page labeled "Give Us Your Feedback". All feedback is collected through this form and sent to you. We do not publish/post feedback on any of our web pages.
