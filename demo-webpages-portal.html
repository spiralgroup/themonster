Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell in the SG MarketPlace?

It's easy to start selling your products and services in the SG MarketPlace. Begin by gathering all your pertinent business information, setting aside 45 minutes, and following these instructions.


1. Create Your Merchant Account

We recommend visiting our live demo so you will know the kind of information needed to create your new Spiral Group, Inc. SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s). Then click here.


2. Select Business Category

Select a category by clicking the image that corresponds to it. To learn more about a category, click on it's label. If you don't see your category, choose the one that most closely characterizes your Small Business.


3. Completing the Order Form

You'll need to provide your business information when you arrive at the online order form. It may help to have this information handy.


3.1 Your Product Images

We prefer that images used for showcasing your products in the SG MarketPlace be of the highest quality.


3.2 Your Staff Photos

You can display individual photos of your staff. Alternatively, you can use a group photo. Images must be of the highest quality.


3.3 Your Special Offers Section

You can create some amazing deals and promotional offers in this section. It may help to determine your offers before signing up to join our marketplace — it will save lots of time. We've developed this handy Amazing Deals Guide to give you an idea about the kind of offers you may want to consider creating for your Special Offers section.


4. Phone Confirmation

Once you've provided the required business and Seller Page(s) information, you'll need to schedule your Phone Confirmation. You'll be contacted by our web developer to complete the process of creating your new Spiral Group, Inc. SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s).

Once we build your Seller Page(s), you’ll receive a unique URL to which you can direct consumers. Your SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s) will be SEO optimized and indexed with search engines, bringing your small business increased traffic and sales.

How will you promote my Seller Page(s)?

We internally promote your SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s), for free, with the following:

• Scrolling Footer Logo Ad. Installed on each SG MarketPlace Seller Page within the same and complimentary business categories.

• The SG Listing Service. We list your small business in only the top eight electronic business directories such as Bing, Foursquare, and Google.

How do I update my Seller Page(s)?

Submit any updates to your Account Manager via email.

Am I a good fit for in-store pickup?

As long as you have a non-residential/non-industrial storefront where customers can pick up their products, then take full advantage of our BOPIS (Buy Online, Pickup In-Store) campaign.

How are returns handled?

We follow whatever the standard return policy is for PayPal. You can review the details in our Merchant Agreement.

How do I update my appointment availability?

You can update your bookable hours whenever you choose. We install Calendly on your SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s). Once we create your free Calendly account, you can begin managing your availability.

How is feedback collected from customers?

There's a "Contact" button located in the menubar on every SG MarketPlace Seller Page. Customers can submit feedback, request quotes, and ask questions about your business. Once we create your free Jotform account, all form submissions are delivered to your inbox.
