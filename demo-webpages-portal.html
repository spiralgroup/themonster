Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell in the SG MarketPlace?

It's easy to start selling your products and services with your Small Business Showcase WebPages. Begin by gathering all your pertinent business information, setting aside 45 minutes, and following these instructions.


1. Create Your Merchant Account

We recommend visiting each of our Small Business Showcase Demo WebPages so you will know the kind of information needed to create your new Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account. Then click here.


2. Select Business Category

Select a category by clicking the image that corresponds to it. To learn more about a category, just click on it's label. If you don't see your category, choose the one that most closely characterizes your Small Business.


3. Completing the Order Form

When you arrive at the online order form, you'll need to provide your business information.


3.1 Your Showcase Images

We prefer that images used for showcasing your products be on a white background. However, if your images don't meet this requirement, upload them anyway and we will do our best to substitute them at no additional charge.


3.2 Your Staff Photos

On your About Us WebPage, you have the option of displaying individual photos of your staff. Alternatively, you can use a group photo. We do not require these images to be on a white background.


4. Net Proceeds

These are the monies you will receive after all costs and expenses are deducted from the Gross Proceeds arising from the sale of your products and services via your Spiral Group, Inc. Small Business Showcase WebPages. You can have your net proceeds delivered through the USPS via check or deposited directly into your bank account.


5. Phone Confirmation

Once you've submitted your completed order form and payment, schedule your Phone Confirmation. Within 24 hours, you'll be contacted by your Account Manager to complete the process of creating your new Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account.

How will customers find my WebPages?

Customers will be able to find your Small Business Showcase WebPages on major search engines when they type the following: Spiral Group + your business name.

How will you promote my small business?

Each SBS WebPages Plan includes a Scrolling Footer Logo Ad, which will be placed on all Small Business Showcase WebPages within the same and complimentary business categories.

Our Premium Plan includes the SG Listing Service — we list your business in the top eight electronic business directories. Among them are VK, Bing, Google, YP.com, and Foursquare.

Our Premium Plan also includes a Branded Category Ad, which will run for 90 days. Feature up to six of your products and services with our scrolling app. These ads will be prominently placed at the top of every qualifying web page throughout the SG MarketPlace.

How do I update my SBS WebPages?

All plans include updates, maintenance, and support. Just submit any updates to your Account Manager via email. He/she will let you know how long it will take to see those changes reflected on your web pages - usually within 24 hours.

Am I a good fit for in-store pickup?

As long as you have a non-residential/non-industrial storefront where a customer can pick up his or her products, then an in-store pickup campaign could be right for you.

How are returns handled?

We follow whatever the standard return policy is for PayPal. You can review the details in our Merchant Agreement.

How do I update my appointment availability?

You can update your bookable hours whenever you choose. We have installed a link to Calendly on each Small Business Showcase WebPage. Log-in to your Calendly account and manage your availability.

How is feedback collected from customers?

On each of your Small Business Showcase WebPages there's a "Contact" link located in the menubar. Using your contact form, customers can submit feedback, request a quote, and ask questions about your products and services. All form submissions are collected and sent to you. We do not publish/post feedback anywhere on our website.
