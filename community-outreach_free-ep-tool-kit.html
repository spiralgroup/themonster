ep tool-kit

CREATED JUST FOR YOU

Spiral Group's Employment Preparation Tool-Kit was designed to teach JOB SEEKERS how to compete in today’s labor market. Participants learn how to set realistic occupational goals; how to conduct occupational research; and how to develop a positive personal brand. As a Training Services Provider, our main goal is to help Job Seekers improve their organizational, decision-making, customer service, and soft skills.

Using our EP Tool-Kit®, along with the Interview Preparation Video Resources, JOB SEEKERS will learn how to make effective use of the skills they already possess and how to communicate these to potential employers. We also offer instruction in telephone etiquette and how to request references from individuals who can attest to a Job Seeker's character and employability.


OVERCOME THE OBSTACLES

Although anyone with a desire to improve their employment readiness skills can benefit from our EP Tool-Kit®, it was originally designed for those with criminal histories. MICHELLE A. JONES [a twice convicted felon] is the Founder & CEO of Spiral Group, Inc. She is dedicated to helping this population of JOB SEEKERS overcome many obstacles to gainful employment.

EP TOOL-KIT® CONTENTS

0 | ORIENTATION COURSE
Investing in your Professional Development is time spent improving your Self, overall. When you complete our online Employment Preparation Training program, you will begin job seeking activities with confidence.

1 | PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT COURSE
This course is comprised wholly of Soft Skills modules—critical thinking, handling workplace conflict, the art of active listening, mindful decision-making, customer service basics, body language, etc. Developing this skill-set is crucial for promoting your success in the workplace.

2 | CAREER DEVELOPMENT COURSE
In this course, we teach you how to conduct occupational research, set occupational goals, and how to begin the process of creating a positive personal brand. There’s also an introduction to entrepreneurship so that you can begin the process of becoming a small business owner.

3 | DOCUMENT PREPARATION COURSE
This course was designed to teach you how to develop your own self-marketing materials [resume, cover letter, job reference page, etc.]. We believe that creating your own documents allows you to know who you are, the skills you possess, and what you can offer an employer. We guarantee this process improves written communication skills.

4 | STRATEGIC INTERVIEW PREPARATION COURSE
In this course, we engage you in modules such as, telephone etiquette for job seekers, strategic interview preparation, appropriate interview attire, and following up after the interview. This course also includes the module LANDING A JOB WITH A CRIMINAL RECORD. It can serve as a guide so the chances of landing a job with a criminal record may increase.

5 | PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT COURSE
This course is presented from the perspective that you have gained employment by convincing the hiring manager of your exceptional capabiities, professionalism, ability to execute the duties of the position, and how you will begin benefitting the company as soon as you are allowed to contribute. This course teaches you how to deliver.

AFTER COMPLETING THIS TRAINING PROGRAM, YOU'RE EXPECTED TO:
 Understand what it means to be JOB READY.
 Demonstrate Soft Skills.
 Conduct occupational and employer research.
 Begin the process of creating a positive personal brand.
 Develop your own self-marketing materials.
 Understand who you are as a candidate and what you can offer an employer.
 If required, address your criminal past with poise, ease, and tact.
 Follow up appropriately after any job interview.

WHO WOULD BENEFIT FROM OUR EP TOOL-KIT®
Job Seekers of all kinds, especially those who have criminal histories.
