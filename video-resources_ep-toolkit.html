Overview

Welcome to the Independent Sales Consultant Resource Portal. The tools and resources you'll need to help our clients grow their small businesses exponentially can be found here. Your primary goal is to work directly with local small businesses to help them achieve their online marketing goals through the SG MarketPlace Seller Pages and our various ad programs.

Your responsibilities include identifying and contacting businesses throughout the tri-state area — Illinois, Indiana & Wisconsin — that would benefit from selling in our marketplace; helping them to determine their needs and business aspirations; customizing every sale to accommodate their wishes; and ensuring their success with our products and services. With each SG MarketPlace sign-up, you've given a small business a chance to get their products and services in front of a larger local audience.

PREPARE FOR SUCCESS

Study our products. If you are able to convey knowledge about our products to customers and answer their questions, they will know you care. If you find our products worthwhile, they will also see the value. As well as getting accurate product information to decision-makers, it is important to translate our product's features into benefits. This makes it easier for the customer to see why they should purchase our products.

Ask questions. If faced with a question you can't answer, refrain from guessing. Instead, tell the customer “I don’t know the exact answer for that, but I’ll be happy to look into it and get back to you. What’s the best way to contact you when I find the answer?”

Be responsive. Send a prompt reply whenever you receive email and voicemail messages. Attend online meetings and conference calls, and engage with colleagues. Communicate your progress, or any challenges you're facing, by scheduling regular check-ins that are convenient for you.

Have a routine. Work at set times, but be flexible. This will also make it easier for colleagues to collaborate with you. Don't work too many hours. Set an alarm to alert you that it's the end of your workday. One of the most rewarding aspects of being a remote worker is your flexibility.

Value time. Time is a valuable resource, but its true value lies in our ability to use it wisely. One way to use time more effectually is by reducing administrative tasks. Know how to avoid spending too much time in spreadsheets; how to untangle yourself from unnecessary paperwork; and when to part ways with a volatile customer relationship management system.

Cultivate healthy habits. Sharp-minded Sales Consultants realize that sitting all day, whether in an office or a car, negatively effects their postures and moods. Take breaks between tasks and move around strategically to clear your mind and de-stress your body. Being present and positive while completing sales-related tasks requires proper sleep. Vow to get between 7-8 hours of sleep every night in order to function optimally.
