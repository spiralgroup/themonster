Shop Online In-Store Pickup is a free service that allows customers to order items online and pick them up in-store at Abt's retail location in Glenview, IL. Only items marked in-stock are eligible for in-store pickup. In-store pickup is only available to local customers during checkout. Certain items are only available for in-store pickup. Purchase Online Once you've chosen your items, you'll go through our regular online Checkout process to purchase your items. Select In-Store Pickup from the available shipping methods in the shopping cart or on the Shipping Information page. Once we've processed your order, we will send you two emails, an Order Confirmation Email that summarizes your purchases and a Store Pick Up Notification Email when your items are ready for pick up. Pick Up In Store Orders will be ready for pick up 1 hour after being placed. By default, the person who placed the order can pick up the items. An alternate pickup person can be specified during checkout by providing the designated individuals first name, last name and, email address. Only the purchaser/designated pickup person can pick up the order. Orders will be held at the store for 7 days. If you have not picked up your order after 7 days, we will cancel the order and refund your card at the end of the holding period. Picking up your order at Abt To ensure your pick up goes smoothly, please bring the following items: A copy of your Store Pickup Notification Email. A valid photo ID (state issued ID or driver's license) The credit card used for your purchase Someone else will be picking up your order You and your designated pickup person will receive a Store Pickup Notification Email when your items are ready for pickup. Your designated pickup person will need to bring the following items: A copy of your Store Pickup Notification Email. A valid photo ID (state issued ID or driver's license) Have any questions or suggestions? Please call 1-888-228-5800.
