Unlike our competitors, we update and completely maintain your Small Business Showcase WebPages. Getting started with us doesn't mean you'll have to attempt to learn code during a 14-day free trial period—unless that's your goal.

You're just minutes away from completing the process of creating your new account. Click the button below for some useful tips.

Creating Your Account

➠ CHOOSING A CATEGORY

Select a business category by clicking the image that corresponds to that particular category. To learn more about a category, just click on the category's label. If you don't see your category, choose the one that most closely characterizes your Small Business.


➠ COMPLETING THE ORDER FORM

Your Small Business Information. Once you have selected your business category, you will be taken to our secure online order form to complete the process of creating your new Merchant Account. Please set aside 30-45 minutes. It may help to have all your pertinent business information handy before you begin. We recommend visiting each of our Demo WebPages so you will know the kind of information needed to complete the online order form.

Choosing Your Plan. If you are not sure which Small Business Showcase WebPages Plan to choose, give us a call. Please keep in mind that our Premium Plan includes the SG Listing Service. If you decide on this plan, do not add the SG Listing Service to your shopping basket.


Your Product Images. We prefer that images used for showcasing your products be on a white background. However, if your images don't meet this requirement, upload them anyway and we will do our best to substitute them at no additional charge.


Team Member Images. On your About Us WebPage, you have the option of displaying individual photos of your team members. Alternatively, you can use a group photo. We do not require these images to be on a white background.


➠ CONFIRMATION

Once we have received your completed order form, we will contact you the next business day to confirm your information. If you have chosen to skip certain fields while completing the order form, we will also use this time to get that information. Please allow time for us to confirm all the details of your new Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account.


➠ OTHER INFORMATION

We require that all plans be paid annually. We do not provide refunds on Small Business Showcase WebPages purchases.
