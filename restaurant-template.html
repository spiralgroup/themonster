Welcome

Our founder, Michelle Jones, had an idea to create a restaurant that was more than a place that served great food. She wanted to give locals a place where they'd feel welcome, could get great food at down-to-earth prices, and where they'd be treated right by people with a passion to serve.

We're loved for our hand-breaded Boneless Buffalo Wings, Broiled Sirloin Tips, frosted mugs of beer, and more. Simple, hearty, delectable food. All served in a distinct Chicago-style and enjoyed with family and friends.

The culture at the SG MarketPlace Restaurant was instilled by Michelle long before a mission statement was ever developed — providing stellar service is our key to success. It's what makes all our guests feel right at home. So, join us for some delicious food and a drink. But most of all, relax.
