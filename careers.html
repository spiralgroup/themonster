We seek creative, energetic, self-motivated, and ambitious people to join our team as Independent Sales Consultants. Persons considered hard to employ, due to a criminal past, are strongly encouraged to apply. Ideal candidates will have exceptional service skills, the drive to be successful, and a teachable mentality.

Independent Sales Consultants work directly with local small businesses to help them achieve their goals through our marketplace and ad programs. You'll be responsible for identifying and contacting businesses throughout Illinois, Indiana, and Wisconsin that would benefit from selling their products in the SG MarketPlace.

Success in this role is measured by meeting and exceeding monthly sales goals, honesty, professional ethics, consistently staying ahead of certain daily metrics, asking probing questions, anticipating customer objections, and dealing with rejection professionally.

Dedicated to Your Success

We provide the resources, training, support, and creative autonomy you'll need to help our clients grow their small businesses exponentially. With each product sold, you've given a local small business an opportunity to get in the game.
