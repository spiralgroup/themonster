Join Our Team

Spiral Group, Inc. is a not for profit organization located in Chicago, Illinois. Our products, services, and ad programs were developed to help locally under-served small businesses increase brand awareness and improve profitability. Because we are a NFP organization, our products and services are offered only to local small businesses that are facing financial difficulties.

We seek creative, energetic, self-motivated, and ambitious people to join our team as Independent Sales Representatives (ISR's). Persons considered hard to employ, due to a criminal past, are strongly encouraged to apply. Ideal candidates will have exceptional service skills, the drive to be successful, and a teachable mentality.

The SG Sales Process

As an ISR, you'll work directly with local small businesses to help them achieve their goals through our various products and ad programs. You'll be responsible for identifying businesses throughout Chicagoland that would benefit from our products and services. Contact them to help determine their needs and business aspirations. Present our catalog in an effort to sell to them. Reasonably customize every sale to accommodate their wishes.


• Developing An Online Presence

Your primary goal is to assist small business owners who need a website with developing an online presence. Therefore, you’ll be responsible for educating small business owners about the value of our Small Business Showcase WebPages. Having an online presence in our marketplace will allow them to achieve many of their business goals, while increasing brand awareness. In this role, you will be directly responsible for selling SBS WebPages Plans according to the needs of each small business.


• Business Excellence

Your next goal is to begin introducing our catalog of Business Excellence Tools, which are designed to assist small business owners in preparing their teams and operational infrastructures so they’re not growing their businesses haphazardly. These tools include customized Mystery Shopping Programs, Mock Health Inspections (for restaurateurs), and Operational Marketing Assessments.


• The Pathway To Success

Once we’ve set up these small businesses to succeed, your next goal is to begin introducing our Local Business Listing Services, and Local Advertising Services. ISR’s are never encouraged to put the horse before the cart. You are required to actually help small businesses succeed and discouraged from attempting to sell to them products and services they do not need. Together, we will determine which products should be suggested to clients based on their level of operational preparedness.

Dedicated to Your Success

You’ll also be working in a consultative sales capacity. You’ll be equipped with all the necessary tools and resources to help our clients achieve their business goals and grow their small businesses exponentially. You’ll work to ensure their teams are adequately trained and well-versed in their company's products and services. You’ll help them prepare their operations to welcome new clients. An online presence is a crucial component of any marketing strategy, but operational excellence will set them apart from competitors.

Success in this role is measured by meeting and exceeding monthly sales goals, honesty, professional ethics, consistently staying ahead of certain daily metrics, asking probing questions, anticipating customer objections, and dealing with rejection professionally.

We provide the resources, training, support, and creative autonomy you'll need to serve our clients in the best way possible. With each product sold, you've helped a local small business in achieving and maintaining operational excellence.
