Let's Grow

We seek creative, energetic, self-motivated, and ambitious people to join our team as Independent Sales Consultants. Ideal candidates will have previous outbound sales experience, exceptional service skills, the drive to be successful, and a teachable mentality. You'll ensure the success of local small businesses by helping them achieve their goals through our online marketplace and various ad products.

You'll be totally responsible for identifying and contacting small business owners by phone throughout Macon County, Illinois who would benefit from selling their products and services in the SG MarketPlace. Success in this role is measured by exceeding sales goals, professional ethics, staying ahead of certain daily metrics, asking probing questions, and anticipating customer objections. Click here to view complete job description.

We provide the resources, training, support, and creative autonomy you'll need to help our clients grow their small businesses. With each product sold, you've given a local small business an opportunity to stay in the game by providing them an affordable opportunity to get their products and services in front of a larger local audience. This is a commission-based sales opportunity. Confident, ethical, and committed Sales Consultants will earn 20% commission on each product sold. To be considered, applicants must be 18 years of age or older. Access to either a laptop or desktop computer is required for this position.

If you don't have immediate access to a laptop or desktop computer, you can visit your local library. Many public libraries allow free access to their computers, and all you'll need is a library card. If you're interested in an enriching opportunity and have what we're seeking, we want to talk to you.
