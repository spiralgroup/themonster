We seek energetic, self-motivated, and ambitious people to join our team as SPIRALGROUP SPECIALISTS. Persons considered hard to employ, due to a criminal past, are strongly encouraged to apply. Ideal candidates will have exceptional service skills and the ability to follow very specific instructions to complete all tasks.


YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES

Identify small businesses that would benefit from our products and services. Contact them and present our products and services in an effort to sell to those customers. Obtain necessary information in an effort to better serve them. If required, set follow-up appointments to close deals.


OUR REQUIREMENTS

Complete our online Professional Development training courses.
Cell phone and reliable transportation.
Previous experience in sales, customer service, or other related fields.
Ability to build rapport with clients and fellow team members.
Ability to think on your feet to communicate potential returns for customers.
Familiarity with Internet terms and use.
Excellent written and verbal communications skills.
Ability to work professionally and collaboratively in a team-setting and while representing Spiral Group, Inc. publicly.


YOUR SALARY

This is a strictly commission-based sales position. You will be working as an independent contractor. We are launching new products and services that will help drive orders and your commissions. Confident, ethical, and committed SPIRALGROUP SPECIALISTS can earn 25% commission on all Spiral Group, Inc. products and services sold to new & existing clients.


WE'RE DEDICATED TO YOUR SUCCESS

We know it takes a special person to work in sales. Therefore, we are committed to providing our team members with an enriching and rewarding environment. We provide the resources, training, and support you'll need to serve our clients in the best way possible. While our performance expectations are high, so is our dedication to your overall success.


WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT

Product Knowledge Training.
Sales Support.
Customer Service & Technical Support.
Marketing Materials.
A Professional Profile On Our Website.
