About Us

Spiral Group, Inc. is a not for profit, 501c3 organization located in Decatur, Illinois. We provide online marketing services for local small businesses. Our product portfolio includes the SG MarketPlace (online and transactional), Mystery Shopping Programs & Services, the SG Business Listing Service™, and SG Local Ad Services™. We're committed to assisting small businesses with increasing brand awareness and improving overall profitability.

We leverage our skills and many years of corporate experience to help small businesses build reliable companies that can support the needs of their markets. In addition, our community outreach program provides free, online employment preparation training for local job seekers with criminal histories.

Schedule a free phone consultation so we can discuss how our products and services can help you achieve your marketing objectives by determining your level of operational preparedness prior to rolling out a new campaign.

Our CEO
Michelle Jones is the Founder and CEO of Spiral Group, Inc. She began developing the company's business model in April 2016 after serving time in a correctional facility. Her goal is to make useful and benevolent contributions to individuals with a criminal past who're willing to improve their circumstances by learning self-sufficiency through our community outreach program.

Michelle became an ordained minister in April 2007. In lieu of a traditional church, she decided to execute her ministerial duties as a web developer and business operations consultant. She has over 20 years' experience in the hospitality and food-service industries as an hourly worker, cashier supervisor, assistant manager, general manager, trainer, training manager, business operations consultant, and ServSafe® Instructor & Proctor. She has worked with the following brands: Subway, Portillo's, Quiznos, Dock's Great Fish, Burger King, Chili's, Target, and Jeepers.

As an Operations Consultant, Michelle evaluated and directed a multitude of franchised restaurants to ensure compliance with company statutes and increase profitability. She assisted franchise owners with attaining managerial and operational control via comprehensive training programs, auditing their financial records, increasing food safety awareness, and preventing health code violations. She also helped them reach local marketing objectives by determining their level of preparedness prior to rolling out campaigns, and by creating a variety of effectual promotional materials.
