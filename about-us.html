WHO WE ARE

Spiral Group, Inc. is a not for profit organization located in Chicago, Illinois. In addition to our SMALL BUSINESS SHOWCASE WEBPAGES, we offer affordable BUSINESS EXCELLENCE TOOLS to locally under-served Small Businesses.

We're committed to assisting this population of entrepreneurs by leveraging our skills and many years of corporate experience to help them build reliable companies that can support the needs of their markets.

THE RETURNING CITIZEN

Michelle Jones is the Founder and CEO of Spiral Group, Inc. She began developing the company's current business model after serving time in a correctional facility. Her goal is to make useful contributions to the Small Business Community.

In 2016, Michelle developed a free, online EMPLOYMENT PREPARATION TRAINING PROGRAM for job seekers with criminal histories. She is dedicated to helping returning citizens in achieving self-sufficiency, while reducing an inclination toward recidivism.


MORE ABOUT US

The purpose of the EMPLOYMENT PREPARATION TRAINING PROGRAM is to educate job seekers with criminal histories on how to overcome the obstacles to gainful employment by improving their organizational, time-management, decision-making, interviewing, and soft skills.

Along her career path, Michelle has worked with the following organizations: Subway, Portillo's, Quiznos, Dock's Great Fish, Burger King, Chili's, Target, and Jeepers. She has over 20 years' experience in the hospitality and food-service industries as an hourly worker, cashier supervisor, assistant manager, general manager, trainer, training manager, and franchise business operations consultant.

Michelle opened her first business, RMRC (Restaurant Materials Resource Center), in 1999 where she designed business documents, labels, and posters for the food-service industry.

She is a Certified ServSafe® Instructor and Registered ServSafe® Examination Proctor. Michelle has been a web developer since 1999, and an ordained minister since 2007.
