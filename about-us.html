the company

the ceo

Michelle Jones is the Founder and CEO of Spiral Group, Inc. She began developing the company's business model after serving time in a correctional facility. Her goal is to make useful contributions to the Small Business Community and other returning citizens.

In 2016, Michelle created a free, online Employment Preparation Training Program for job seekers with criminal histories. She is dedicated to helping returning citizens in achieving self-sufficiency, while reducing an inclination toward recidivism.

Minister Michelle Jones

Business Consultant ∣ Web Developer

Michelle became an ordained minister in April 2007. In lieu of a traditional church, she decided to execute her ministerial duties as a web developer and business operations consultant through her not for profit organization - Spiral Group, Inc. The NFP was established in April 2016, and offers affordable online marketing services (Small Business Showcase WebPages & Local Business Listings Service) and business assessment tools for small businesses throughout the greater Chicagoland area. In recent months, she has adapted the NFP's business model to provide lucrative job opportunities to persons considered hard to employ, due to a criminal past.

Michelle has over 20 years' experience in the hospitality and food-service industries as an hourly worker, cashier supervisor, assistant manager, general manager, trainer, training manager, and business operations consultant. She has worked with the following brands: Subway, Portillo's, Quiznos, Dock's Great Fish, Burger King, Chili's, Target, and Jeepers.

Michelle opened her first business, Restaurant Materials Resource Center, in 1999 where she designed business documents, labels, and posters for the food-service industry. She is a Certified ServSafe® Instructor and Registered ServSafe® Examination Proctor. As a Franchise Field Operations Consultant for the Subway brand, she evaluated and directed 25-34 Subway restaurants, each month, to ensure compliance with company statutes and to increase overall profitability.

Michelle has assisted franchise owners with achieving managerial and operational control via comprehensive training programs, auditing their financial records, and enforcing personal hygiene policies. She conducted monthly health inspections, in each of the 25-34 restaurants, to increase food safety awareness and prevent health code violations. In addition, she assisted the franchisees in her territory with achieving their Local Store Marketing objectives by determining their level of preparedness prior to rolling out their campaigns.
