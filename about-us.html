the returning citizen

Michelle Jones is the Founder and CEO of Spiral Group, Inc. She began developing the company's business model after serving time in a correctional facility. Her goal is to make useful contributions to the Small Business Community and other returning citizens.

In 2016, Michelle created a free, online EMPLOYMENT PREPARATION TRAINING PROGRAM for job seekers with criminal histories. She is dedicated to helping returning citizens in achieving self-sufficiency, while reducing an inclination toward recidivism.

Taking a Break from Web Development

Along her career path, Michelle has worked with the following organizations: Subway, Portillo's, Quiznos, Dock's Great Fish, Burger King, Chili's, Target, and Jeepers. She has over 20 years' experience in the hospitality and food-service industries as an hourly worker, cashier supervisor, assistant manager, general manager, trainer, training manager, and franchise business operations consultant.

Michelle opened her first business, RMRC (Restaurant Materials Resource Center), in 1999 where she designed business documents, labels, and posters for the food-service industry.

She is a Certified ServSafe® Instructor and Registered ServSafe® Examination Proctor. Michelle has been a web developer since 1999, and an ordained minister since 2007.
