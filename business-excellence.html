MYSTERY SHOPPING PROGRAM

We design and implement mystery shopping programs that provide honest and in-depth views from the customers' perspective. We compile and analyze data from completed evaluations to gain awareness about how it affects your small business, operationally.

Our insights include wait & service times; impact of workforce training; quality of products & services; suggestive selling techniques; and parting statements.


×

HOW THIS WORKS

PLAN

First, we'll get to know your small business and operational structure so we can devise a custom mystery shopping plan, and create a specific timeline.

SCHEDULE

Next, we schedule certified shoppers to perform your specific tasks, such as purchasing & returning items, calling your customer service line, using your website, or simply asking questions of your associates. After discretely completing your tasks, our shoppers move on to the next phase.

EVALUATE

During this phase, the shopper completes the questionnaire we created specifically for your project. Answers are a combination of multiple choice or scored points, along with highly detailed comments that allow you to fully understand the overall customer experience.

ANALYZE

We compile and analyze data from completed evaluations to gain insight into how it affects your small business, and present you with a view of your business through the eyes of your customers. We offer recommendations on how to honor stellar performance, or improve where needed.

IMPROVE

We guide you on how to improve overall operational strategies using what we learn from the evaluations. Your new knowledge of problem areas will help you create noticeable changes that will delight your customers.

REPEAT

We will work with you to create a schedule of ongoing evaluations and other customer experience research services that will help fine-tune your brand experience and take your small business to the next level.
