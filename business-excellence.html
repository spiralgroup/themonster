Mystery Shopping Program

Schedule Free Consultation

We'll customize a Mystery Shopping Program that will help identify operational challenges, recognize the high achievers among your workforce, and incline your small business toward excellence. We train our shoppers to deliver a comprehensive, professional, and unbiased view of your operation that will allow you to understand how customers experience your brand.

*Insights Include

• Sales Force Effectiveness

• Impact of Team Training

• Suggestive Selling Techniques

• Opening & Parting Statements

• Staff Appearance

• Friendliness & Engagement

• Operational Compliance

• Overall Quality of Products & Services

• Product/Service Selection & Availability

• General Appearance of Location

• Wait & Service Times

• Condition & Functionality of Restroom(s)

*These insights are meant to provide a general idea about the kind of information many organizations seek to acquire when they hire a mystery shopping company. When we begin customizing your mystery shopping program, you will decide on which elements of your operation are to be evaluated.

How It Works

1. Plan

We get to know your business so we can devise a custom plan and create a specific timeline of execution. The cost to conduct each shop is determined during this step.

2. Schedule

We schedule shoppers to discretely perform your tasks: purchasing & returning items, calling your service line, using your website, asking specific questions of your staff, etc.

3. Evaluate

The shopper completes your questionnaire. Answers are a combination of multiple choice, scored points, and comments that allow you to understand the overall customer experience.

4. Analyze

We analyze data from your evaluations to gain insight into how it affects your small business.

5. Improve

We provide specific recommendations that will enhance the overall customer experience.

6. Repeat

We create a schedule of ongoing evaluations and help take your business to the next level.

mock health inspection

It is likely that every organization in the food industry will undergo regular public health inspections conducted by local government health departments. We can help you prepare for an official City of Chicago Health Department Inspection.

During your Mock Health Inspection, we work closely with you to ensure food safety, while prioritizing any missed opportunities within your last health inspection report.

×

Mock Health Inspection

Schedule Free Consultation

Our Mock Health Inspections are an independent review of your food service operation. During the inspection, we pay particular attention to the factors that could lead to potential Health Code Violations. We help you understand the underlying causes of food safety problems and recognize staff that may need additional training.

At the conclusion of the inspection, we review the results with the person in charge. Within 48 hours you receive a comprehensive report which includes a detailed summary of the inspection, specific recommendations for correcting violations, and information on how to develop and install a *Food Safety Plan. Our goal is to help increase the chances of receiving a higher grade during a follow-up City of Chicago Health Inspection.


*A set of written documents based on food safety principles used to identify hazards that require preventive controls to prevent foodborne illness or injury. It incorporates hazard analysis, preventive controls, a recall plan, and delineates the procedures to be followed for monitoring, corrective actions, and verification.

Operational Marketing Assessment

Schedule Free Consultation

The Operational Marketing Assessment can help determine your company's level of preparedness before rolling out your next marketing campaign. It enables you to increase the effectiveness of your marketing efforts by identifying the strengths and weaknesses of your business systems; by examining how workflow and structure affect customer service; and by understanding how your small business performs against competitors.

The most important element of a successful operational marketing strategy is supporting it. Your company's marketing tactics must fit with your team's ability to assist in achieving your objectives. Once we have determined your company's level of preparedness, we develop your Strategic Management Plan. It will contain recommended actions to take within your small business in order to reduce operating costs, increase profitability, improve workforce productivity, and enhance customer service.
