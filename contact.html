PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

BUSINESS OPERATIONS ASSESSMENT

Using the Business Operations Assessment, we're able to provide a critical assessment of your entire operation, including elements contributing to the overall customer experience. After thorough analysis, we are able to troubleshoot your business systems and determine your level of operational readiness.

Once this is accomplished, we can create a Strategic Management Plan, which will guide us in the direction of reducing costs, increasing profitability, and improving workforce productivity.

ASSESSMENT COMPONENTS

1. Information gathering sessions.
2. Interviews with leadership team.
3. Competitive, market, operational, financial, and SWOT analyses.
4. Strategic Management Plan or S-M-P.

We work collaboratively with the theme of your operation, including your vision of success as a small business owner.
