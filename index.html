Keep Your Dream Alive

Don't give up on your dream because COVID 19 did not come to stay — it came to pass. It presented itself so that we can take a closer look at our business operations, standards, and practices. If we remain honest with ourselves, we will begin to see the tasks we must undertake and the real goals we need to accomplish. Don't allow the appearance of devastation kill your small business.

Begin by consciously choosing a healthy and small-business-oriented perspective. This is an opprtunity to rid our businesses of things, practices, people, policies, etc. that have outlived their usefulness and purpose. Small businesses can begin using this time to create practical action items. Below are some action item examples.

• Retrain staff

• Prepare to recruit, hire, onboard, and train new employees

• Examine operational infrastructure in an effort to determine what needs to be eliminated and what needs to be expanded

• Eliminate excessive menu and inventory items

• Determine a more effective pricing strategy

• Review standard operating procedures or SOP's

• Develop a new, more sensible marketing plan

• Develop daily, weekly, monthly, yearly cleaning and maintenance plans

• Update your existing websites

• Remain active, positive, realistic, and dedicated throughout this process
