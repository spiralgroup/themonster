How It Works

Getting Started

After clicking an Add Listing or Get Started button anywhere on our website, you will arrive at the page that allows you to begin the process of creating your new Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account.


Choosing Your Category

Select a business category by clicking the image that corresponds to it. To learn more about a category, just click on it's label. If you don't see your category, choose the one that most closely characterizes your Small Business.


Completing the Order Form

➠ Your Small Business Information. Once you have selected your business category, you will be taken to our secure online order form to continue the process of creating your new Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account. Please set aside 30-45 minutes. It may help to have all your pertinent business information handy before you begin. We recommend visiting each of our Demo WebPages so you will know the kind of information needed to complete the online order form.

➠ Choosing Your Plan. If you are not sure which Small Business Showcase WebPages Plan to choose, give us a call. Please keep in mind that our Premium Plan includes the SG Listing Service. If you decide on this plan, do not add the SG Listing Service to your shopping basket.

➠ Your Product Images. We prefer that images used for showcasing your products be on a white background. However, if your images don't meet this requirement, upload them anyway and we will do our best to substitute them at no additional charge.

➠ Team Member Images. On your About Us WebPage, you have the option of displaying individual photos of your staff. Alternatively, you can use a group photo. We do not require these images to be on a white background.


Phone Confirmation

Once you have submitted your completed order form and payment, you will need to schedule your Phone Confirmation to complete the process of creating your Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account.
