Getting Started

After clicking an Add Listing, Get Started, or Choose Plan button anywhere on our website, you will arrive at the page that allows you to begin the process of creating your new Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account.


Choosing Your Category

Select a business category by clicking the image that corresponds to it. To learn more about a category, just click on it's label. If you don't see your category, choose the one that most closely characterizes your Small Business.


Completing the Order Form

1. Your Small Business Information. Once you have selected your business category, you will be taken to our secure online order form to continue the process of creating your new Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account. Please set aside 30-45 minutes. It may help to have all your pertinent business information handy before you begin. We recommend visiting each of our Demo WebPages so you will know the kind of information needed to complete the online order form.

2. Your Product Images. We prefer that images used for showcasing your products be on a white background. However, if your images don't meet this requirement, upload them anyway and we will do our best to substitute them at no additional charge.

3. Team Member Images. On your About WebPage, you have the option of displaying individual photos of your staff. Alternatively, you can use a group photo. We do not require these images to be on a white background.

4. Your Net Proceeds. These are the monies you will receive after all costs and expenses are deducted from the Gross Proceeds arising from the sale of your products and services via your Spiral Group, Inc. Small Business Showcase WebPages Merchant Services account. You can choose to have these monies delivered through the USPS via check or deposited directly into your bank account.


Phone Confirmation

Once you have submitted your completed order form and payment, you will be sent to a page that will allow you to schedule your Phone Confirmation. After scheduling your Phone Confirmation, you will be contacted by our web developer to complete the process of creating your Spiral Group, Inc. Merchant Account. It is important to confirm the information submitted to ensure your web pages reflect the details you want communicated about your brand.

We will also use this time to fill in any blanks or make adjustments to the information you initially submitted. Phone Confirmations are conducted Sunday through Saturday, between 11am and 7pm (this includes major holidays). This process should take approximately 15 minutes to complete.
