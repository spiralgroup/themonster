What Will Happen Next

We will contact you at the phone number provided to conduct your Phone Confirmation. It is important to confirm this information to ensure your SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s) reflect the details you want communicated about your brand.

If you chose to skip some of the fields on the order form, we'll use this time to fill in those blanks and make adjustments to the information you initially submitted. Depending on how many items you submitted for your SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s), this process should take no more than 45 minutes.

Once we have confirmed your SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s) Account information, your pages will go live within 7 business days and you'll receive a unique URL for your seller page(s) via email. You'll also receive account information for the following applications installed on your seller page(s): Jotform, Mailerlite, and Calendly.


Other Information

Through our SG Listing Service, we will begin listing your small business information in the top electronic business directories immediately after your SG MarketPlace Seller Page(s) go live. Afterwards, you will receive your account information for the following online business directories: Better Business Bureau (local), Bing, ChiBiz Hub, Foursquare, Google My Business, Local.com, VK.com, Yelp, and YP.com


Inform your Account Manager of any Seller Page(s) updates via email. Changes should be reflected within 48 hours.


When it comes to your Special Offers section, let our handy Amazing Deals Guide inspire you to create some of the most amazing deals, special offers, and discounts. Once again, thank you for being a Seller in the SG MarketPlace.
