In this section, we provide the details of your product or service. We retreive this data from your completed and verified order form. The name of your product or service, along with the price, is installed directly beneath the corresponding image. In addition, an Add to Cart button is installed inside the modal for ease of purchase. Each of your items has a modal for this purpose.

Include your specific instructions on how customers are to retrieve their online purchases from your retail location. We have provided an example below.

Once you've chosen your items, you'll go through our regular online Checkout process to purchase them. Select "In-Store Pickup" from the available shipping methods in the shopping cart or on the Shipping Information page. Once we've processed your order, we will send you an Order Confirmation Email that summarizes your purchases and a Store Pick Up Notification Email when your items are ready for pick up.

Orders will be ready for pick up 1 hour after being placed. By default, the person who placed the order can pick up the items. An alternate pickup person can be specified during checkout by providing the designated individuals first and last names, and email address. Only the purchaser or designated pickup person can pick up the order. Orders will be held at the store for 7 days. If you have not picked up your order after 7 days, we will cancel the order and refund your card at the end of the holding period.

You Will Need:

 A copy of your Store Pickup Notification Email.

 A valid photo ID (state issued ID or driver's license).

 The credit card used for your purchase.


Your Designated Person Will Need:

 A copy of your Store Pickup Notification Email.

 A valid photo ID (state issued ID or driver's license).


If you have questions, please call 773-242-0705.
