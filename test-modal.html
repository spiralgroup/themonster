Spiral Group, Inc. is a Not for Profit organization located in Chicago, Illinois. We provide Small Business Showcase Webpages and other affordable Business Excellence Tools for locally under-served small businesses. We believe in a diverse and inclusive environment, one that is grounded in our dedication to supporting the Small Business Community.

We seek creative, energetic, self-motivated, and ambitious people to join our team as Independent Sales Representatives. Persons considered hard to employ, due to a criminal past, are strongly encouraged to apply. Ideal candidates will have exceptional service skills, the drive to be successful, and an understanding of the sales process and its related dynamics, or the teachable mentality to learn it.

You'll be responsible for identifying Small Businesses throughout Chicagoland that would benefit from our products and services. Contact them and present our catalog in an effort to sell to those customers. Obtain necessary information in an effort to better serve them. Set follow-up appointments to close deals.

We provide the resources, training, support, and creative autonomy you'll need to serve our clients in the best way possible. With each product sold, you've helped a local small business in achieving and maintaining operational excellence.
