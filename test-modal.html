Product Description

In this section, we provide the details of your product or service. The name of your product or service, along with price, is installed directly above its corresponding image. Each of your items has a modal for this purpose.

Buy Online, Pick Up In-Store

Include your specific instructions, in this section, on how your customers are to retrieve their online purchases from your retail location. We have provided the following example.

BOPIS INSTRUCTION EXAMPLE

Once you've chosen your items, you'll go through our regular online Checkout process to purchase them. Select "In-Store Pickup" from the available shipping methods in the shopping cart or on the Shipping Information page.

Once we've processed your order, we will send you an Order Confirmation Email that summarizes your purchases and a Store Pickup Notification Email when your items are ready for pick up.

Orders will be ready for pick up 1 hour after being placed. By default, the person who placed the order can pick up the items. An alternate pickup person can be specified during checkout by providing the designated individuals first and last names, and email address. Only the purchaser or designated pickup person can pick up the order.

Orders will be held at the store for 7 days. If you have not picked up your order after 7 days, we will cancel the order and refund your card at the end of the holding period.


You Will Need:

⚬ A copy of your Store Pickup Notification Email.
⚬ A valid photo ID (state issued ID or driver's license).
⚬ The credit card used for your purchase.


Your Designated Person Will Need:

⚬ A copy of your Store Pickup Notification Email.
⚬ A valid photo ID (state issued ID or driver's license).


If you have questions, call 773-242-0705.
