Step 1. Choose Your Plan

Review each plan carefully to make sure it meets your current business needs. All plans include showcase pages maintenance & dedicated customer support. Once you've selected a plan, you will automatically proceed to Step 2.

Step 2. Choose a Business Category

The SpiralGroup MarketPlace has 12 business categories. For details about a single category, tap on the category label. Once you've decided on a category, tap on that category's image and proceed to Step 3.

Step 3. Provide Your Business Information

In this Step, you will need to provide all the pertinent details of your small business. Please fill out the form, in its entirety, to avoid delays with publishing your showcase pages.

Step 4. List Your Showcase Items

List all the items you want showcased. If you don't have any product images, please inform your SpiralGroup Specialist. You may be able to borrow from our library of stock photos for a nominal fee.

Step 5. Proceed to Checkout

In this step, provide your payment information using our secure PayPal® account. If you don't have a credit or debit card, then give us a call so that we can discuss other payment options.

Step 6. Get Verified

We will review your information for compliance with our guidelines, and then contact you by phone to confirm and verify the data. Once approved, your showcase will go live within 72 hours.
